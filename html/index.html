How it happened?

Hmm... I don't belong to the older generation, nor to the young. I'm mostly self-taught, having been passionate about how computers work since I was a kid. I took my first one apart when I was about 8 years old. Well, it didn't survive the operation, but at the time I knew what would interest me for the rest of my life.

There are no coincidences in life. You have to earn everything yourself, whether by paying with time or money. I learn useful skills on my own. (Need to know now, click here) Sometimes I have a lack of desire and motivation, but once I get into something, nothing can stop me.

My free time...

Sure, you’ve met the coder in me — but in my downtime, I tinker with game consoles, sketch stuff, and, well… some say my “dark side” is that I’m a furry - 🦊. It might raise a few eyebrows when people peek into my cozy little nook — but once you get to know me, you’ll know I’ve got your back.

Outside of work, I like to chill in my little nook with some single-player games — and when it’s time to socialize, I’m usually at the table playing Pokémon TCG with friends.

These days, you’ll also find me helping out in the Mikrus project — mostly as support, but hey, every weird user question is another chance to poke around and learn something new. My nook’s knowledge shelf keeps growing thanks to that!

Even a fox needs to step out of the nook sometimes. For over 6 years, I’ve been helping run the WOŚP team at my old technical school. No code, just people, energy, and lots of good vibes. It’s wild in its own way — and I love being part of it.
